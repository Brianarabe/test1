FAQ

What is the process of buying a property?

Buying a property typically involves determining your budget, getting pre-approved for a loan, searching for properties, making an offer, conducting inspections, and closing the deal with legal documentation.

How much should I budget for a down payment?

Most buyers pay between 10% to 20% of the property price as a down payment. However, this can vary depending on the loan type and location.

What are the additional costs when purchasing a property?

Aside from the property price, you should expect costs like taxes, legal fees, registration fees, insurance, and maintenance expenses.

How do I know if a property is a good investment?

A good property investment depends on location, market trends, potential rental income, infrastructure development, and long-term value appreciation.

Can foreigners buy property in the Philippines?

Foreigners can legally own condominium units but cannot own land. They can, however, lease land long-term or invest through corporations with Filipino ownership requirements.
